Project Goal

Create a timeless visual identity that communicates calm mornings, slow living, artisan coffee, indoor plants, and Scandinavian-inspired interiors.

Brand Story

Root & Reverie was born from the belief that life should be slower, greener, and more intentional. Customers enjoy specialty coffee while surrounded by lush indoor plants and warm natural textures. The brand combines Scandinavian minimalism with botanical elegance to create a peaceful escape from busy city life.
